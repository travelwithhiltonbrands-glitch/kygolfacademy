Hi, I’m Kevin Yoon, a golf professional at Pinecrest Golf Course. For over a decade, my goal has been simple: to make learning golf easier and more enjoyable.

My coaching approach isn’t the traditional one. I bring an engineering background and analytical mindset to help golfers understand their swing and improve more efficiently—while keeping drills and concepts simple and easy to apply for players of all ages.

I grew up in Blacksburg, Virginia, a small college town in the Blue Ridge Mountains. At age 17, I was introduced to golf by one of my closest friends and met PGA Professional Steve Prater, who became one of the most influential mentors in my life.

Within a year, I became a single-digit handicap player and earned multiple top-10 finishes in tournaments across the country. In high school, I competed on a varsity team that won three consecutive Virginia state championships.

My path eventually led me into academics, where I studied business and engineering at George Washington University and Cornell University. In 2010, I earned my teaching license through the United States Golf Teachers Federation.

Today, I teach at Pinecrest Golf Course in Alexandria, Virginia, helping golfers understand not just what they do—but why—so improvement becomes consistent and lasting.

Everything I’ve built in golf has been possible with the full support of my loving mother, whose encouragement has been a constant throughout my journey.
