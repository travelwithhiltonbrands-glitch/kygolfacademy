Hi, I’m Kevin Yoon, a golf professional at Pinecrest Golf Course. For over a decade, my goal has been simple: to make learning golf easier and more enjoyable.

My coaching approach isn’t the traditional one. I bring an engineering background and analytical mindset to help golfers understand their swing and improve more efficiently while keeping drills simple and easy to apply.

I grew up in Blacksburg, Virginia...

I earned multiple tournament finishes and competed on a team that won three state championships.

I studied at George Washington University and Cornell University and became a certified instructor in 2010.

I now teach at Pinecrest Golf Course in Alexandria, Virginia.

Everything I’ve built in golf has been possible with the full support of my loving mother.
